About Spokane

Spokane is the economic and cultural center of the Inland Northwest region. Incorporated in 1881, Spokane is known for its beautiful parks and outdoor activities.

Population: 228,989 (2020)

Region: Eastern Washington

Classification: Urban

Average Income Level: Lower than the state average
