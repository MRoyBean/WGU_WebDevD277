About Olympia

Olympia, the capital city of Washington State, is located in the western part of the state. It was incorporated in 1859 and serves as a hub for state government activities.

Population: 52,555 (2020)

Region: Western Washington

Classification: Urban

Average Income Level: Higher than the state average

Highlights

  • Home to the Washington State Capitol
  • Known for its parks and waterfront
  • Rich in history and culture
