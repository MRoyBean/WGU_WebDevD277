About Seattle

Seattle, known for its iconic Space Needle and vibrant culture, is a major seaport city in Washington State. It was incorporated in 1869 and is a center for technology, music, and coffee culture.

Population: 737,015 (2020)

Region: Western Washington

Classification: Urban

Average Income Level: Higher than the state average

Highlights

  • Home to major tech companies like Amazon and Microsoft
  • Renowned for its music scene
  • Features landmarks such as Pike Place Market and the Space Needle
